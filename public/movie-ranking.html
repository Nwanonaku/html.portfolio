All Time Best Movies According To Ifeanyi

Top 3 Movies

1. BloodLine

This felt so real to me while watching it. It had my eyes glued to the screen all day till the end of each season. Too bad season 2 was not as good

2. Merlin

Wow! Who else remembers how sensational this series was? Gwendoline, Author and Morgana. These guys killed it for me, perfect combo.

3. Party Planner

This one from Nigeria was another realistic commedy featuring Brother Shaggy. If you really want to laugh out loud this one is for you.
